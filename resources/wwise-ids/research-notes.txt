-------- START NPC PREFIX DOCS


Only NPC weapons have prefix. FPS weapons are in their corresponding soundbank.

Structure for weapons is:

AUTOFIRE:
[tweak_data.weapon[WEAPON].sounds.prefix] + 
user_sound_variant (hardset to 1 on RayCastWeaponBase, but used to be 1, 2 or 3) +
self._voice (can be "a", "b" or "c") +
{nr_shots and "_" + tostring(nr_shots) + "shot", otherwise "_loop"} (nr_shots is either 1, 2, or 3)

AUTOFIRE END:
[tweak_data.weapon[WEAPON].sounds.prefix] + 
user_sound_variant (hardset to 1 on RayCastWeaponBase, but used to be 1, 2 or 3) +
self._voice (can be "a", "b" or "c") +
"_end"
